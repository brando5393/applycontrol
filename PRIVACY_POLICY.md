# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-02-27

ApplyControl is a job application tracking extension. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

## Data We Collect
We collect the minimum data needed to provide the service:
- **Account data**: email address and user ID (via Firebase Authentication).
- **Application data**: job posting URL, title, timestamps, status, and any extracted fields such as company, location, and description.
- **Feedback data**: title and message you submit in the feedback form, plus basic device metadata (browser user agent, platform, app version, and extension version).

## How We Use Data
- Provide and improve core features (saving applications, viewing your dashboard, status tracking).
- Support and troubleshooting (feedback entries and metadata).
- Security and abuse prevention.

## Data Sharing
We share data only with:
- **Google Firebase** (Authentication and Firestore) as our infrastructure provider.

We do **not** sell your data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

## Data Retention
We retain data until you delete it (e.g., removing application entries) or request deletion.

## Security
All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Firebase with access rules that restrict access to the authenticated user.

## Your Choices
- You can delete your application entries.
- You can delete your account and all associated data (applications and feedback).
- You can choose not to submit feedback.

## Contact
If you have questions, open a feedback ticket from within the extension.

## Limited Use Statement
If we access any data from Google APIs, we will only use it to provide or improve ApplyControl’s core functionality and will comply with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
